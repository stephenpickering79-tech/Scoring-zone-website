## Design 3: Drill Explainer — Lag King

**Instagram:**
The drill that kills 3-putts: Lag King.

Here's how it works:

1. Set up from 30+ feet — 10 balls at 30, 35, and 40 feet from the hole
2. Hit 10 lag putts — focus on speed, not line. Leave every ball within a 3-foot circle
3. Count your makes — how many finished within 3 feet? That's your score
4. Beat the benchmark — 6/10 = Under 10 HCP level. 8/10 = Under 5. 10/10 = Tour Pro

Speed control is the #1 skill that separates amateurs from scratch golfers. This drill trains it in 10 minutes.

Scoring Zone tracks your Lag King score across sessions. Watch yourself improve.
